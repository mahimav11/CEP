About Campus Care

Campus Care: a smartphone-based complaint management system designed to integrate the user interface with the maintenance team. Maintenance requests such as civil, electrical, air conditioning, water supply, telephone, or network issues on the campus are quickly resolved.

Users can lodge a request/complaint through text/pictures input.

For instance, to report a problem with a tubelight in an unnamed location, the location can be recorded using the GPS-based location feature on the smartphone.
